Landing Page Title

To build a globally connected, equitable, and innovation-driven ecosystem that supports leadership in the impact world in order to connect and synergize efforts across the global South and otherwise.

Our Vision

To be the world's leading research, innovation and social enterprise hub, for promoting diversity and inclusion of under-represented communities, by designing sustainable developmental frameworks.

We do that by bringing governance and programming experts to enable multi-sectoral collaboration to inform systems change that can advance DEI in organizations and communities.

To create the largest ecosystem of emerging leaders from across sectors and communities who will build and scale ventures or champion causes that lead to deep, sustainable, and equitable impact.

Our core services capitalize on systems-change approaches to advancing equity that move from a focus on individuals to a focus on the systems that shape outcomes from birth to career.

Our Values

Radical Empathy

We listen to learn. Building trust with each other and those we serve is core to our mission.

Progress over Perfection

We are a learning organization that takes an iterative approach to developing our team and our solutions.

Lasting Contributions

We are systems-thinkers. We aim to contribute meaningful and sustainable solutions.

Equity for All

We hold ourselves and our partners accountable to a process and result that is transparent and equitable.
